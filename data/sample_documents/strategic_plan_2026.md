# Strategic Plan 2026

## Executive Summary

Acme Corp's 2026 strategic plan centers on three pillars: digital transformation,
market expansion, and talent development. The board approved a $48M investment
across these initiatives, with measurable KPIs tied to each quarter.

## Pillar 1: Digital Transformation

The technology modernization program will migrate legacy on-premises systems
to a cloud-native architecture by Q3 2026. Key milestones include:

- **Q1:** Complete infrastructure assessment and vendor selection
- **Q2:** Migrate core ERP and CRM platforms to AWS
- **Q3:** Launch customer-facing digital portal
- **Q4:** Achieve 99.9% uptime SLA and retire legacy data centers

Expected ROI is 3.2x over 36 months, primarily from reduced infrastructure costs
and improved developer productivity.

## Pillar 2: Market Expansion

Acme will enter the Asia-Pacific market through a joint venture partnership
with Tanaka Industries. The expansion targets $12M in new revenue by Q4 2026.

Regional offices will open in Singapore (Q1) and Tokyo (Q2), with a localized
product offering tailored to enterprise clients in financial services and
healthcare.

## Pillar 3: Talent Development

The Chief People Officer will oversee a leadership development program for
150 high-potential managers. The program includes:

- Executive coaching (12 sessions per participant)
- Cross-functional rotation assignments (90 days)
- AI literacy certification for all senior leaders by Q3 2026

Employee retention target: 92% for top quartile performers.

## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|------------|
| Cloud migration delays | High | Medium | Parallel running period, rollback plan |
| APAC regulatory changes | Medium | Low | Local legal counsel retained |
| Key talent attrition | High | Medium | Retention bonuses, accelerated promotion paths |

## Budget Allocation

Total investment: $48M
- Digital Transformation: $28M (58%)
- Market Expansion: $14M (29%)
- Talent Development: $6M (13%)
